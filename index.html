Matt Wright


Product Design

Since early 2021, I’ve worked at Mercury as a Senior Product Designer. I’m leading design for the iOS and Android apps. On the side, I’m focused on design outreach and design team culture

From 2020 to 2021, I worked at ODX as a Product Designer under the leadership of Gabby Manotoc. I built out a white-labeled design system that allowed our product to be personalized for various clients such as PNC, UBS, and Huntington Bank. ODX merged with Fundation to create Linear Financial Technologies – I worked alongside Lauren Gallagher and Gabby Manotoc to create the brand identity. Linear has since been acquired by Amount.

Years prior to Mercury and ODX, I worked as a UX Design Intern at Limeade.


Freelance Practice

On the side, I maintain a freelance practice called Internet Design Company. I partner with tech companies, design studios, and individuals. My practice specializes in Web and App Design, Design Audits, Design Systems, Digital Brand Design, and Product Strategy.

I’ve worked with various clients such as Pfizer, ADP, Deloitte, Barstool Sports, BibleProject, and Kalpa.

In May of 2022, I worked with a former colleague to create wireframes to launch the MVP of a product called Kalpa – a clinical trial software that streamlines collaboration between pharmaceutical companies, providers, and administrators.

In October of 2021, I partnered with Malka Media to create a microsite for ADP. The microsite was a interactive questionaire designed for c-level executives who could benefit from learning about the numerous ways their business could be disrupted.

From early to mid 2021, I partnered with Fivestone in an Art Direction and Design capacity to build out a design system for BibleProject. After establishing an art direction and a color and type system, I oversaw a handful of designers who carried the system to completion.
