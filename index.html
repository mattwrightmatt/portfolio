Matt Wright

Hi, my name is Matt. I'm a designer based in Seattle, Washington. I've been designing the Mercury app for about five years now. Before that, I was at Team and Malka Media, designing for clients like Pfizer, ADP, and Deloitte.

I originally intended to pursue Industrial Design because of the tangible impact physical objects have on people’s everyday lives. However, after discovering the Interaction Design program at the University of Washington, I fell in love with the discipline. Interaction Design gave me the flexibility to solve problems in any way that resonated with my target audience the most – whether the deliverable was physical, digital, or print. I learned to conduct user research, apply first principles thinking to my findings, and create beautiful things that solved problems.

Outside of work, I spend my time with my partner, our dog, and many projects. My home espresso hobby, house projects, and reading fills my downtime and helps me feel connected to the world around me.
