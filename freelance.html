On the side, I maintain a freelance practice called Internet Design Company. I partner with tech companies, design studios, and individuals. My practice specializes in Web and App Design, Design Audits, Design Systems, Digital Brand Design, and Product Strategy.

I’ve worked with various clients such as Pfizer, ADP, Deloitte, Barstool Sports, BibleProject, and Kalpa.

In May of 2022, I worked with a former colleague to create wireframes to launch the MVP of a product called Kalpa – a clinical trial software that streamlines collaboration between pharmaceutical companies, providers, and administrators.

In October of 2021, I partnered with Malka Media to create a microsite for ADP. The microsite was an interactive questionnaire designed for c-suite executives who could benefit from learning about the numerous ways their business could be disrupted.

From early to mid 2021, I partnered with Fivestone in an Art Direction and Design capacity to build out a design system for BibleProject. After establishing an art direction and a color and type system, I oversaw a handful of designers who carried the system to completion.

In July 2020, I partnered with Malka Media to design a landing page for a Barstool Sports sports betting app.

In June 2020, I partnered with Malka Media on a quick turnaround projet to design a microsite for attendees of the Deloitte Best Managed Companies conference.

In February 2020, I partnered with Malka Media to design an ADP Evolution of Pay microsite that visualized complex payment method data across different regions and age groups around the world.

For the first few months of 2020, I partnered with Malka Media to design an educational microsite to demo the Next Gen HCM app. The microsite design provided a framework that inspired the design for ADP demos across multiple departments.
